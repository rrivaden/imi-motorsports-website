Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to make a reservation?

Go-Kart racing is available on a first-come, first-served basis during regular operating hours. For weekends and holidays, we recommend arriving early as wait times can be longer.

For all other tracks and special events, we highly recommend making a reservation to ensure track availability, especially during peak times and weekends.

How can I book a group or corporate event?

For group or corporate events, please call (303) 833-4949 ext. 2 or email events@imimotorsports.com. Our events team will help you customize the perfect experience for your group.

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance to ensure availability, especially during peak season (April-October).

What's your cancellation policy?

For individual bookings, cancellations made 48 hours or more before your scheduled time will receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours may receive a credit for future use.

For group events, our cancellation policy varies based on group size and event type. Please refer to your booking agreement or contact our events team for specific details.

Do you host birthday parties?

Yes! We offer special birthday packages for all ages. Our packages include track time, dedicated party area, and options for food and beverages.

For more information or to book a birthday party, please call (303) 833-4949 or email events@imimotorsports.com.
