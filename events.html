Events FAQs

How far in advance should I book my event?

For smaller groups (under 10 people), we recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance. For larger groups or corporate events, we suggest 4-6 weeks advance booking to ensure availability and proper preparation.

During peak season (April-October), all events should be booked further in advance as our calendar fills up quickly, especially for weekend dates.

Can I bring my own food and drinks for my event?

For birthday parties and small group events, you may bring your own cake and non-alcoholic beverages. For all other food and drink items, we offer catering options through our approved vendors.

Corporate events have additional catering options available. Please contact our events team for a full menu of options.

What is the cancellation policy for events?

For most events, cancellations made at least 14 days in advance receive a full refund of the deposit. Cancellations made 7-13 days in advance receive a 50% refund. Cancellations less than 7 days in advance forfeit the deposit.

Weather-related cancellations may be rescheduled without penalty at the discretion of management. We will always work with you to find a suitable alternative date.

How do I register for a race event?

Race event registration can be done through our online registration portal, by phone, or in person at our facility. Each race event has specific requirements and entry fees, which are listed on the event details page.

Registration typically closes 24-48 hours before the event start time. Some popular events may sell out early, so we recommend registering as soon as possible.

Can I host a fundraising event at IMI Motorsports?

Yes! We welcome fundraising events and have special packages designed for non-profit organizations. Our facility provides a unique venue that can help drive attendance and participation for your fundraiser.

Please contact our events team directly to discuss your fundraising goals and how we can help you create a successful event.
