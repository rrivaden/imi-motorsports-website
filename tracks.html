Frequently Asked Questions

What age requirements are there for racing?

Go-Karts: Drivers must be at least 8 years old and 50" tall to drive solo. Children 5-7 who are at least 40" tall may ride with an adult in our double karts.

Motocross Track: Riders must be at least 6 years old with appropriate sized motorcycles and safety equipment.

Quarter Midget Track: Available for children ages 5-16 with proper racing equipment and parental supervision.

Off-Road: Drivers must be at least 16 years old with a valid driver's license to rent UTVs. Riders bringing their own vehicles must follow age-appropriate guidelines for their specific vehicle type.

Do I need to make a reservation?

Go-Kart racing is available on a first-come, first-served basis during regular operating hours. For weekends and holidays, we recommend arriving early as wait times can be longer.

For all other tracks and special events, we highly recommend making a reservation to ensure track availability, especially during peak times and weekends.

Group events and corporate functions require advance booking with a deposit.

What should I wear for racing?

For Go-Kart racing: Closed-toe shoes are required. Comfortable clothing that allows freedom of movement is recommended. Helmets are provided.

For Motocross: Full protective gear is required, including DOT-approved helmet, goggles, long sleeve jersey, pants, gloves, boots, and body protection.

For Off-Road activities: Closed-toe shoes, long pants, and weather-appropriate attire. Helmets are required and provided with rentals.

For Circle Track and Quarter Midget racing: Approved racing suit, helmet, neck restraint, gloves, and appropriate safety equipment for the specific racing class.

Are there weather restrictions?

IMI Motorsports operates in most weather conditions, but certain tracks may close during heavy rain, lightning, or extreme weather for safety reasons.

Go-Kart racing continues in light rain but may be paused during heavier precipitation.

Off-Road courses often remain open in wet conditions, adding to the challenge and experience.

We recommend calling ahead if weather conditions are questionable.

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes! Gift certificates are available for all our experiences and make excellent gifts for motorsport enthusiasts. They can be purchased online or in person at our facility.

Gift certificates are valid for one year from the date of purchase and can be applied to any activity or merchandise at IMI Motorsports.
