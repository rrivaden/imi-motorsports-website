About IMI Motorsports

Home / About Us

Our Story

IMI Motorsports is Colorado's premier motorsports complex, offering a variety of racing experiences for enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels. Located in Erie, Colorado, our facility spans across acres of specially designed tracks and terrain to provide the ultimate motorsport playground.

Since our founding, we've been dedicated to creating an accessible, world-class environment where drivers and adrenaline lovers can experience the thrill of motorsports while achieving their performance driving goals.

Our Mission

To provide an exceptional motorsports environment that inspires passion for racing, fosters skill development, and creates unforgettable experiences for all motorsport enthusiasts.

Our Team

Our dedicated staff of racing enthusiasts and professional instructors are committed to delivering safe, exciting, and educational motorsport experiences for visitors of all skill levels.

Our Standards

We maintain the highest standards of safety, track maintenance, and customer service to ensure every visit to IMI Motorsports is exceptional, memorable, and worth returning for.

World-Class Facilities

IMI Motorsports offers comprehensive facilities designed to accommodate various racing styles and motorsport activities.

Professional Asphalt Track

Our one-mile asphalt track features professional-grade surfaces, challenging turns, and long straightaways for maximum speed and precision racing.

State-of-the-Art Go Karts

Experience the fastest go kart rentals in Colorado with our regularly maintained fleet of high-performance karts suitable for racers of all skill levels.

Dynamic Motocross Track

Our motocross track offers jumps, berms, and technical sections that challenge riders while providing an exhilarating off-road experience.

Visitor Amenities

Enjoy our comfortable viewing areas, pit spaces, parking facilities, and on-site amenities designed to enhance your motorsport experience.
